Tutoring Experience

Skills and Background

Since February 2025, I have been working part-time as a Mathematics tutor for a first-year engineering student at the University of Johannesburg. I focus on core subjects like Calculus and Algebra, using one-on-one sessions to match the student's pace and learning style. I help explain difficult topics such as limits, derivatives, integrals, linear equations, matrices, and conic sections in a way that’s easier to understand. I create practice exercises, visual aids, and revision materials that target the student’s needs and support their learning. My approach has helped improve their academic performance and confidence in solving math problems. This role has also helped me improve my ability to explain complex ideas and adapt to different ways people learn.

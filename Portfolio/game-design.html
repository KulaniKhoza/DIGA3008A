Game Design Portfolio

2D PLATFORMER GAME

This 2D platformer prototype challenges players to navigate a side-scrolling environment using precise movement and jumping mechanics. The primary goal is to collect coins scattered throughout the level, with each coin contributing to a visible score counter. The game features two levels that test the player's timing, precision, and strategic thinking. In Level 1, players must move across varied terrain and reach elevated platforms to collect coins. In Level 2, gameplay intensifies with the introduction of moving pipe obstacles, requiring players to time their jumps carefully to pass through narrow gaps and collect mid-air coins. Collision with obstacles results in a reset to the last checkpoint. The game is designed to maintain a careful balance between challenge and fairness, encouraging replayability while providing a rewarding sense of progression.

Unity C# 2D Platformer

FARMING SIMULATOR

The Farming Simulator game is a 2D interactive experience focused on strategic resource management within a farm environment. Players begin by purchasing items from a shop using limited in-game currency, including seeds and animal feed, which are added to their inventory and used to interact with the game world. Seeds can be planted on plowed soil, and animals like chickens can be fed to produce eggs that generate income. Players must balance spending on various activities such as plowing land, planting crops, and feeding animals, as overspending in one area can stall overall progress. The economic system encourages thoughtful decision-making, where successful actions lead to rewards and level-ups that unlock further gameplay opportunities. The core challenge lies in maintaining an efficient and sustainable farming operation while gradually expanding and upgrading through smart planning and timely execution.

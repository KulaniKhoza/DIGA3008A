Electronic Project

Echoes of manchine

This subsystem uses LDRs ,an Ardiuno uno microcontroller and a Neopixel ring. The LDRs detect the intensity of the light from the Mic system and outputs the result in patterns on the Neopixel ring varing on the intensity. The PIR sensors detect direction of motion "of the birds" and display light on the neopixel ring in direction of the motion as well as on the array of leds. The Ultrasonic sensor detects a nearby hand acting as a bird, triggering a green NeoPixel animation. This visually represents the bird's (hand's) presence in the nest.

How it works

  • The LDRs measure light intensity, influencing the NeoPixel patterns.
  • PIR sensors detect motion direction, illuminating the NeoPixel ring accordingly.
  • The ultrasonic sensor detects a nearby hand, triggering a green NeoPixel animation.
  • For more information: GitHub Repository: Echoes of a Machine

Simulation

  • Open wokwi.com to view the simulation of the project ,press the button below.
  • Click play to start the simulation.
  • Toggle the Photoresistors to see how the NeoPixel ring responds to different light intensities.
  • Toggle the PIR sensors to see how the NeoPixel ring responds to motion.(click the PIR sensors to toggle them click both of them to see the full effect)
  • Toggle the Ultrasonic sensor to see how the NeoPixel ring responds to a nearby hand.

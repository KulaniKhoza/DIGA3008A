Style Guide

Goal and Aesthetic: I envision my portfolio website with a clean, neutral color scheme, using muted tones to keep users focused on the content without distraction. I aim for a modern, welcoming aesthetic with user-friendly navigation and clear, bold typography wiyh a clear theme. The site should maintain a professional look to appeal to potential employers and with a creative and unique style that grabs attention and reflects my personal approach.

1. Visual Aesthetic & Design Process

This phase focuses on defining the visual identity by choosing fonts, colors, and layout structures that align with the intended user experience.

1.1 Fonts – Readability & Legibility

Possible Choices:
  • Sans-serif fonts (e.g., Inter, Roboto, Montserrat) for a clean, modern look.
  • Headings: Montserrat Bold (for emphasis)(Havard website)
  • Serif fonts (e.g., Merriweather, Playfair Display) for a professional or elegant feel
  • Button text: All caps with medium, bold in white to grab user attention
Implemetation and fonts used

The website’s typography is designed for both impact and readability. the Heading to create strong visual emphasis, reflecting a modern and professional tone inspired by the Harvard website. Body text chosen for its clean and highly legible style that ensures smooth reading. Button text is styled in all caps with a bold white font, helping calls-to-action and stand out.

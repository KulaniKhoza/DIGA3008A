Essay 1: UI/UX

The essay explores the ethical considerations of UI/UX design by analyzing the Capitec Bank website. It discusses how privacy and transparency are maintained through cookie authorizations, secure login processes, and clear data usage, fostering user trust. Inclusivity and neutrality are addressed through simple language, language options, and clear navigation, though the site lacks audio support for visually impaired users. The website avoids dark patterns, with no misleading links or aggressive advertising, although frequent prompts to “open an account” suggest subtle marketing. Overall, the website reflects ethical UI/UX design principles, offering a secure, user-friendly, and visually clean experience, with minor areas for improvement.

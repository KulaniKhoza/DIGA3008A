Close-Reading:

Close reading is a way of analyzing literature that pays close attention to the details within a passage or text. By examining these elements carefully, the reader uncovers deeper meanings and insights that may not be immediately apparent. This method encourages readers to engage with the text on a more profound level, fostering a deeper understanding of the author's intentions and the work's overall significance.From blogs 9 and above this type of method is used to show my improvement in Critical reflection and Analysis
